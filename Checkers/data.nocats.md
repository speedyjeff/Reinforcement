### Dis-incentivize Cats
This is a training run of two learning opponents where the rewards for finishing are winning(+1), losing(-2), and cats(-1).  The training used the following initial setup.

```
White goes first (down) and should win.
   0  1  2  3  4  5  6  7
0  -  -  -  w  -  -  -  -
1  b  -  w  -  -  -  W  -
2  -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
3  -  -  b  -  -  -  -  -
4  -  b  -  -  -  -  -  -
5  -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
6  -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
7  -  -  -  -  -  -  -  -
```

#### Take-aways
 * Dis-incentivizing cats games increased the number of unique contexts by nearly 10x.
 * The outcome was still more often than not a cats game.
 * This is the right default.
